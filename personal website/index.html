All About Dolphins

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and acrobatic displays. They belong to the family Delphinidae and can be found in oceans and seas all around the world.

Interesting Dolphin Facts

  • Dolphins use echolocation to navigate and hunt for food.
  • There are around 90 different species of dolphins.
  • Some dolphins can reach speeds of up to 20 miles per hour (32 kilometers per hour).
  • The largest dolphin species is the Orca, or Killer Whale.
  • Dolphins are social animals and live in groups called pods.

Top 5 Dolphin Species

  1. Bottlenose Dolphin
  2. Common Dolphin
  3. Spinner Dolphin
  4. Atlantic Spotted Dolphin
  5. Risso's Dolphin
